Payment Verification Help
Main Functionality covered by Payment:
  1. Payment: To view the payment which is already paid.
  2. New Payment Entry (offline Payment Modes): Payment done at office counter.
  3. Payment Verification (Online Payment Modes): To verify online payments.
  4. View Summary 1: To view summary 1 report.
  5. Payment Receipt: To view the receipt copy.
  6. New Payment Heading: To add New Payment Heading or to increase in fee amount.
  7. Adjustment Amount: Amount adjusted by fee exemptions or from old document.
  1. Payment
  2. This amount is entered from New Payment Entry or Payment Verification is available under Payment and along with details shown like Amount to be paid, paid amount and balance amount along with its grand total and Payment head wise.
  3. Amount entered from New Payment entry will not be verified as it is offline payment mode and so edit and delete functionality provided.
  4. Amount entered from Payment Verification is verified as it is online payment mode and so edit and delete functionality not available.
  1. New Payment Entry(offline Payment Modes):
  2. New Payment Entry is the payment done at office counter, here entire Fee details provided according to various payment head in right handside tabular format which will be more convinient to user to do Payment Process. The offline payment mode(Ex:stamp paper,Demand Draft/cheque,Cash,Bank Challan) are available.
  3. Requirements of Offline Payment modes are
      1.Stamp paper : Stamp Number,Purchase Date,Amount,Account Head.
      2.Demand Draft(DD)/Cheque: Bank Name,Bank Branch Name,DD Number,IFSC Code,Payment Date,Amount,Account Head.
      3.Cash: Payment Date,Amount,Account Head.
      4.Bank Challan: Bank Name,Challan Date,Challan Amount,Bank Transaction Id,Account Head.
      If saved through Offline payment mode then Edit and Delete functionality is avaialble.
  1. Payment Verification (Online Payment Modes):
  2. Online Payment Mode is to verify online payments and verification process is completed by using an electronic medium like E-Stamp,E-Registration, and GRAS which are verified and configured state wise. The online payment like GRAS/E-stamp which is verified through web service and added.Further it is automatically defaced/locked after completion of STAMP-2/STEP-2 of Registration Process.
  3. Requirements of Online Payment modes which are Verified:
      1.E-stamp: e-Stamp Issue Date,Certificate Number->Here if verified then appears on the first tab i.e Payment
      2.E-Registration: Certificate Number.
      3.GRAS: GRN Number,GRN Date,GRN Amount
      If saved through Online payment mode then Edit and Delete functionality is disabled.
  1. View Summary 1
  2. Click on View Summary 1 to view Document Registration View Summary 1 Report.
  1. Payment Receipt
  2. Click on Payment Receipt to view the receipt copy,and this Payment Receipt available as original and customer copy for download and print format too.
  1. New Payment Heading
  2. To add New Payment Heading or to increase in fee amount.
  1. Adjustment Amount
  2. Adjustment Amount is nothing but the amount which is adjusted by fee exemptions or adjusted amount from old document,Here SRO can remove/delete this added adjustment amount in any of the cases observed invalid.
