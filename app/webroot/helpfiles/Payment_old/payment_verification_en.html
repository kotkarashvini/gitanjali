Payment Entry Help
  1. Payment tab
  2. The amount saved from New Payment Entry tab and Payment Verification tab and available on Payment tab and here payment is verified according to the amount to be paid,paid amount,Balance amount with Payment head.
  1. New Payment Entry tab-New Payment Entry (offline Payment Modes):
  2. Payment Verification tab-Payment Verification (Online Payment Modes): Here in New Payment Verification various details are added newly like payment mode and is corresponding dependent various fields.and for reference purpose the amount to be paid are displayed according to the payment head wise
  3. Various Payment modes available like
      1.Stamp paper : Stamp Number,Purchase Date,Amount,Account Head.
      2.Demand Draft(DD)/Cheque:Bank Name,Bank Branch Name,IFSC Code,Payment Date,Amount,Account Head.
      3.E-Registration:Certificate Number,Certificate Date,Amount,Account Head
      3.Cash:Payment Date,Amount,Account Head.
      3.Bank Challan:Bank Name,Challan Date,Challan Amount,Bank Transaction Id,Account Head
  1. Payment Verification tab-Payment Verification (Online Payment Modes):
  2. The verification process carried out by two methods like E-Stamp,E-Registration, and GRAS which are verified through web service and also which is configured state wise.
  3. Various Payment modes available like
      1.E-stamp (Verified though web service ): e-Stamp Issue Date,Certificate Number
      2.E-Registration(Verified though web service ):Certificate Number.
      3.GRAS (Verified though web service ):GRN Number,GRN Date,GRN Amount
  1. View Citizen Data tab
  2. Here Entire Citizen data is available like Payment Mode,Payment Head,Payer Name,Amount Paid and Click on View Citizen Data to view entered the details.
