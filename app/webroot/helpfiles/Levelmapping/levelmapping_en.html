Location Level Mapping Help
  • Notice : It is compulsory to enter information in the column that is required (*).
  1. LEVEL 1 MAPPING:
    2.
  2. Select the 3 dropdown list like :District,Taluka,Village
  3. According to the selection done the level 1 will be displayed.
  4. LEVEL 1:>
  5. A>Click on PLUS(+) SIGN to view the page to fill the data.
  6. C>Enter level 1 details according to the corresponding language specified.
  7. D>Select survey or door details and submit the form.
  8. D>The added data will be displayed in the grid.
  9. LEVEL 1 LIST:>
  10. A>Click on Edit symbol of level 1 grid to edit the the before data of level 1 and also to view the page named level 1 list to fill the data on clicking on plus (+)sign.
  11. C>Enter level list 1 details according to the corresponding language specified.
  12. D>Enter the Range from and Range to value and say add to save the level 1 list.
  13. D>The added data will be displayed in the grid.
